1984 Redux
Introducing 1984 Redux

This is my extensive revision of the original "1984" Summerboard theme for iPhone OS 1 by Chris Reiff ("Mr_Sparkle"). It's a gorgeous 1-bit theme designed to make your iPhone 2G or iPod Touch 1G look like a Macintosh from 1984!

All the theme's existing icons have been tweaked or redrawn entirely (at 2x scale) to remove inconsistencies and scaling artifacts. Many of the newly included icons incorporate elements from classic Mac OS apps and UI graphics. The dock, menubar, and wallpaper images have been redrawn from scratch.

This will work on a jailbroken iPhone or iPod Touch with Summerboard or Winterboard installed. Refer to the readme file for instructions.
